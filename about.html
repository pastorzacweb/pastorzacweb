His Story

About
Pastor Zac

Pastor Zac Akinadewo was born with a heart for the Word of God. A divine calling to preach the Gospel was made clear in his youth, and he surrendered fully to full-time ministry.

Pastor Zac Akinadewo is a Nigerian preacher called to declare the Word of God with boldness and authority. Over more than two decades in ministry, he has preached at conferences, crusades, and assemblies across Nigeria and beyond — carrying a consistent message of the Kingdom of God, revival, and discipleship.

"But you have come to Mount Zion, to the city of the living God, the heavenly Jerusalem. You have come to thousands upon thousands of angels in joyful assembly, to the church of the firstborn, whose names are written in heaven."– Hebrews 12:22-23

He is the founder of The Outpouring Ministry and convenes the annual Fire Conference — a gathering that has seen thousands encounter the living God. His ministry is marked by the demonstration of the Holy Spirit, healing of the sick, and the salvation of souls.

"After this I will return and will rebuild the tabernacle of David, which has fallen down; I will rebuild its ruins, and I will set it up; so that the rest of mankind may seek the LORD, even all the Gentiles who are called by My name."– Acts 15:16-17

Pastor Zac is a devoted family man. He believes the Church is the hope of the world and that every believer is called to be a carrier of the Gospel. His ministry exists to fan that flame — in pulpits, through media, and in the lives of everyday people.

"But they that wait upon the Lord shall renew their strength; they shall mount up with wings as eagles; they shall run, and not be weary; and they shall walk, and not faint."– Isaiah 40:31
"For the kingdom of God is not in word, but in power."– 1 Corinthians 4:20
