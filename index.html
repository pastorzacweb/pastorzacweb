Proclaiming the Kingdom

Dr. Zacchaeus Akinadewo (DBA, FCA, FIMC, FCTI, MBA), widely known as Pastor Zac, is the Lead Pastor of Word of Faith Chapel (MCCSW Word of Faith Chapel), Abuja, Nigeria. A Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria and a distinguished professional, he is equally called as a gospel music minister. A multi-instrumentalist who plays the keyboard, guitar, drums, and saxophone, his music blends contemporary African worship with deep, spirit-filled sound, carrying thousands into sincere praise and intimacy with God.
