About the Ministry

The Outpouring is a spirit-led movement dedicated to propagating the worship of Jesus Christ across the world. Born out of Pastor Zac Akinadewo's passion for the body of Christ, this ministry hosts the presence and glory of God through prophetic worship and the preaching of the Word.

What began as a local gathering has grown into a movement stretching across Nigeria and reaching believers in multiple nations through live streams and recordings.

The Outpouring

A live, non-denominational revival meeting that holds as led by the Holy Spirit. Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt are among the cities where The Outpouring has been held.

Each gathering is marked by extended worship, the preached Word, and the miraculous presence of the Holy Spirit.

Fire Conference

The annual Fire Conference is a 3-day intensive gathering of ministers, believers, and seekers who come together to seek God, receive the Word, and be ignited for service.

Past conferences have featured impartation, healing services, and powerful nights of worship.
