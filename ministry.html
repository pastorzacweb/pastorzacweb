Life Changing Ministry

Word of Faith Chapel, also known as MCCSW Word of Faith Chapel, is a vibrant Christian ministry under the Cherubim and Seraphim Worldwide, committed to raising lives through the undiluted teaching of God's Word.

The church is built on the foundation of faith, spiritual revelation, and disciplined Christian living. It carries a clear mandate to nurture believers into maturity, guiding them towards a life of purpose, orderliness, and unwavering trust in God.

At Word of Faith Chapel, the focus is on transforming lives through consistent teaching, prayer, and practical application of biblical principles not just hearing the Word, but living it daily, producing visible change in the lives of its members.

"Behold, how good and how pleasant it is for brethren to dwell together in unity! For there the LORD bestows his blessing, even life forevermore." – Psalm 133:1, 3

Sunday

9:00 AM

Thanksgiving Service

Thanksgiving Service

Every Sunday at 9:00 AM, the congregation gathers for a powerful time of corporate thanksgiving, praise, and worship. This service is the heartbeat of the church a moment where believers come together to acknowledge God's faithfulness, receive the Word, and be strengthened for the week ahead.

The Thanksgiving Service sets the tone of gratitude and expectation, reminding every member that God is worthy of all praise and that He continues to work on behalf of His people.

Join Us Sunday

Tuesday

6:00 PM

Word Meditation

Word Meditation

The Tuesday Word Meditation session is a dedicated mid-week gathering where the Word of God is not just read it is meditated upon, digested, and allowed to take root deep in the hearts of believers.

As Joshua 1:8 instructs, meditating on the Word day and night is the pathway to prosperous and successful living. This gathering provides a focused atmosphere for believers to slow down, reflect deeply, and allow the transformative power of Scripture to shape their thinking and daily decisions.

Join Us Tuesday

Thursday

6:00 PM

Faith Exposition

Faith Exposition

The Thursday Faith Exposition is an in-depth, systematic teaching session designed to build and strengthen the faith of every believer. Drawing from the riches of Scripture, Pastor Zac unpacks the practical dimensions of faith what it means to believe God, how to speak in faith, and how to walk by faith in every area of life.

Hebrews 11:1 declares that faith is the substance of things hoped for. This gathering exists to ensure that every member understands, grows in, and lives out this vital principle producing tangible results in their families, careers, and spiritual lives.
